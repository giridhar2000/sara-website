SANS PAREIL IT SERVICES LTD

5 Placed In Off Campus Drive By SANS PAREIL IT SERVICES LTD

Sans Pareil IT Services conducted an off campus placement drive at PABCET on 9th Nov 2013. Out of the more than 1500 students from 10 different colleges who participated in the drive, 195 students were from our college. After the initial Aptitude test round 66 students (36 for non-technical + 30 for technical) were shortlisted for the interview round. 20 of these students were from our college. 80% results in the Aptitude was taken as the cut-off point for qualifying for further rounds.

25 students for non-technical and 5 students for technical were given offer letters that evening.

3 out of the 25 selected for Non-Technical were from our college. They are Ms.Rashmi .B (IT), Ms. Smrithi.A (IT), & Mr. Ambarisha Varadhan M (MBA))

2 out of the 5 selected for technical were from our college. They are Ms. Deepti A (CSE) and Mr. Hariharan (MCA).

"Our congratulations to the successful candidates and our best wishes for a great and bright career ahead"

ONE-GLOBE OFF CAMPUS

Two Selected For One Globe From Off Campus Drive On 17th Dec 2013

An Off Campus Placement Drive For The CSE, IT & MCA Graduates of 2014 was conducted on 17th December 2013 by the company One-Globe at JJ College.

A total of 151 (56 from our college, 26 from Mookambigai and 69 from JJ) students participated in the drive.

After the initial Aptitude test round 38(17 from our college, 8 from Mookambikai and 13 from JJ) students were shortlisted for the next round of technical test.

A total of 9 (3 from each of the participating colleges)students cleared this round and took part in the GD round.

4 were finally selected from this round and took part and cleared their HR and personal interview rounds.

The Final HR Round has been completed over phone and two from our college - Ms.Jasmine Terrancia and Ms.Deebika R (both from CSE) - have been made job offers with joining dates as 1st September 2014 .

Congratulations are due to the successful students and hope they would bring further laurels to the college at the work spot and be true ambassadors to our institution.

CTS OFF CAMPUS

34 Students From Our College Have Been Placed At CTS OFF CAMPUS

The CTS Off campus drive was conducted by Anna University at Kongunadu College, Thottiyam on 20th, 21st and 22nd 2013.

134 colleges participated with approx 3400 students. From our college 105 students participated.

The selection process was GD first, Aptitude Test next followed by Technical HR and Personal HR interviews.

After the GD round and Aptitude Test 420 students from the 134 colleges were shortlisted for the Technical and HR rounds. With 50 shortlisted students at this stage our college was the highest among the 134 colleges which participated. The next best is National College with 34 (counting both their colleges) followed by Sethu colle ge with 32

Finally 229 Students From The Madurai Zone Have Been Placed. Of This 34 Students Are From Our College.

List Of 34 Students From Our College Have Been Placed At CTS OFF CAMPUS Click Based on number of students we are the 1 st on the list of 134 colleges from the Madurai Zone.The next two colleges are Sethu College with 18 students and National College of Engineering with 14 students in the final list of placed students.

These excellent results should make all of us proud and unite to congratulate the successful students. These results should also make us ponder over the following points:

1. If the students perform well it does not matter whether the drive is off campus or on campus. This result is better than Infosys which was an on-campus drive.
2. Careful and meticulous preparation by way of special client focused training sessions for any such drive will certainly yield results.
3. If the feedback from the participating students is any indication then the extensive training sessions provided by Happemiles, LaunchPad, Smart & FACE at various levels to the students have certainly come in handy and have yielded results.

It is now up to these successful students to spread this message to the juniors in our college and impress upon them the need and use of these training programs.

DIGITAL NIRVANA

1 Selected, 6 Shortlisted By Digital Nirvana LaunchPad organised a pooled campus drive at our college for the company Digital Nirvana on 30th Dec 2013.

304 students (187 from our college, 69 from SASTRA, 34 from JJ, 9 from CARE, 2 each from Ramakrishna & Trichy Engineering and 1 from Angalamman) participated in the drive.

After the initial address the students were asked to take a paper and pen English grammar test. only 27 students cleared this round. 12 students each from our college and SASTRA and 3 from JJ were the ones short listed at this stage.

They were then asked to take an audio comprehension session followed by an essay session.

After these two sessions only 5 from SASTRA and 7 from our college were short listed for the HR rounds.

After the HR rounds 2 from SASTRA and K N Harish from our college were selected and handed over offer letters. the remaining 3 from SASTRA and 6 from our college are expected to take one more round of telephonic interview before their selection is confirmed.
