Bus Facilities

The College has a fleet of buses to facilitate the to and fro transport of students. A fleet of twenty five buses are operated from different parts of the neighbouring areas to bring the students and staff to the college premises in time. In addition, a number of state owned and private town and mofussil buses ply via the college and this place is easily accessible from Tiruchi Bus stand and Railway station. The Government of Tamilnadu has arranged an exclusive bus to ply between Mainguard gate and our college, both in the morning and evening.

Boys Hostel

The boys hostel is in a separate spacious and airy 3 storied building, is located within the college campus. Available facilities include:



01.Spacious, well ventilated and well lighted rooms with cots, tables, chairs and fans.
02.Hygenic kitchen and dining hall where wholesome vegetarian food is served.
03.Library with large number of text and reference books.
04.Generation backup to ensure uninterrupted power, supply during study hours.
05.Mini computer center with 10 Mbps internet connectivity.
06.Wi-fi facility.
07.Recreation hall.
08.Pure drinking water from RO plant.
09.Solar water heater for hot water during winter.
10.Playgrounds of the college near the hostel.

Girls Hostel

The girls hostel, run by the college, though not located within the college campus, is just 6 KM away from the college on the Trichy-Viralimali national highway. The hostel can accommodate 250 students. The available facilities are:



01.Well ventilated, well lighted rooms and spacious rooms with cots, tables, chairs and fans.
02.Pure drinking water from RO plant.
03.Generation backup.
04.Mini computer center with 10 Mbps internet connectivity and Wi-Fi connectivity.
05.Recreation hall.
06.Hygenic kitchen and dining hall serving wholesome vegetarian food.
07.Sports facilities.
08.Bus facility to transport the students from Hostel to college and back.
09.24-Hour all round security service to ensure safety of inmates.

Canteen

Canteen facilities with a dining room annexe are available at the Campus and quality vegetarian food is served during the college days.

Separate STD booth with Xerox facility and a Stationery Store are available for the benefit of students.

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
