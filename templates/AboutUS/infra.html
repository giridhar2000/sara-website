Infrastructure at SCE


With a built-up area of 200000 square feet, 8 seminar halls, and a state of the art library with more than 40000 books and 225 periodicals and journals (both national and international), the college offers the ideal environment for the students to absorb and assimilate the top-class technical education and professional training offered.



Secretary K.Santhanam Block
The imposing Main Block named `SECRETARY K.SANTHANAM BLOCK has three floors covering an area of 77,000 sq. ftIt accommodates classrooms, tutorial rooms, seminar halls, laboratories for the Electrical Department, Electronics and Communication Department, and Instrumentation and Control Engineering Department.

RV Block & Kamaraj Block
The RV Block covering an area of 76,500 sq.ft. It accommodates classrooms, tutorial rooms, seminar halls, and Common laboratories for the Computer Science and Engineering Department, Information Technology Department, First Year common courses and it also accommodates administrative offices.

Jagadguru Sri Jayendra Saraswathi Block
The Jagadguru Sri Jayendra Saraswathi block covering an area of 25,300 sq. ft It houses the Civil Department and its Laboratories, Library and Conference Hall.

 SCE

Saranathan College of Engineering was founded by “Vidya Seva Ratnam”, “Guru Seva Mani” Auditor Sri. K. Santhanam in the year 1998. It was so named in respectful memory of his Guru Principal Saranathan, the greatest ever Principal of National College. It is a self financing college approved by AICTE and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai.
